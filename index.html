De Mayo Reaction: Core Mechanistic Pathways

Staple Photochemical [2+2] Cycloaddition - Unchanged for 60+ Years

Classic Pathway: β-Hydroxy Enone
  • 1 Starting: β-Hydroxy enone + Alkene
  • 2 [2+2] Photocycloaddition: Occurs at 3,4-position, forms cyclobutane
  • 3 Retro-Aldol Ring Opening: β-OH facilitates cleavage
  • 4 Product: 1,5-Dicarbonyl (Linear, versatile)
Variant: Enaminone Pathway
  • 1 Starting: Enaminone + Alkene
  • 2 [2+2] Photocycloaddition: Same mode, cyclobutane
  • 3 Retro-Mannich Opening: N-involvement in breaking
  • 4 Product: Ketoimine (C=N functionality)

✓ Supporting Evidence for ESIPT

  • • No fluorescence at RT - fast deactivation
  • • Z-isomer stabilized by intramolecular H-bonding

  • • This rules out pathways not requiring H-transfer
